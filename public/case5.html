Case 5: Required Documents

  2. Copy of your Passport
  3. Proof of Residence (utility bill or similar)
  4. Proof of Income (pay stub or tax form)
  5. Medical Certificate
  6. Proof of Enrollment (if student)
  7. Signature Authorization Page
